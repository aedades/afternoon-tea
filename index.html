Tea Service

Earl Grey
A classic black tea with distinctive bergamot citrus notes, creating a bright and aromatic cup with floral undertones.
Darjeeling
A delicate black tea with muscatel notes and a light, refined character that won't overpower subtle flavors.
Jasmine Green
A fragrant green tea scented with jasmine flowers, offering floral aromatics and a clean, refreshing finish.
Rooibos (Red Bush)
A naturally caffeine-free herbal tea with vanilla and honey notes, offering a warm, sweet, and gentle cup.

Accompaniments: honey, oat milk, soy milk

Menu Progression

1Savory Finger Sandwiches

Paper-thin cucumber slices with fresh butter on soft white bread - a British classic
